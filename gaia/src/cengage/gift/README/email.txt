Summary of changes:
 
GPMS-166: GIFT-Refactor GIFT Schema
Solution: See “GIFT_3.0_ReleaseNotes.docx”
 
GPMS-174: GIFT - addition of time elements to metadata.xsd
Solution: 
metadata.xsd

    Created new elements meta:publication-time, meta:standard-time and meta:composed-time.
    Added meta:publication-time and child elements meta:standard-time and meta:composed-time in meta:publication-date

 
Example: 
<meta:publication-date>
        <meta:structured-date>
                <meta:irregular>December 5, 2013</meta:irregular>
        </meta:structured-date>
        <meta:standard-date>20131205</meta:standard-date>
        <meta:publication-time>
                <meta:standard-time>08:00:00-05:00</meta:standard-time>
                <meta:composed-time>0800EST</meta:composed-time>
        </meta:publication-time>
</meta:publication-date>
